About ferranhead

Ferran Gimeno (ferranhead) was born and raised in València, Spain, and currently lives and works in London. After graduating in Advertising, he has developed a skill set with a core in web design, motion graphics, digital arts, and 3D (CGI).

After spending 4 years in two advertising agencies in Madrid working as a creative and designer, he decided to move to London in 2012, where he changed the agency environment for the design studio.

Experience

My interest in design started as a teenager, learning to code HTML in Notepad. I sharpened these skills while attending San Diego State University by learning the fundamentals and principles of design. Core concepts such as gestalt psychology were fascinating. Over the last two years, I've shifted focus to UX Design. Whether I'm sketching, wireframing, prototyping, the field combines my love of psychology with my passion for design.
